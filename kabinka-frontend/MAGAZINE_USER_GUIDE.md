# Magazine Feature - Quick Start Guide

## Overview
Magazine is a curated digital advertising platform within Kabinka Social. It's a standalone feature where users can browse editorial-style ads from local creators and businesses.

## Accessing Magazine

### From Navigation Drawer
1. Open the app
2. Tap the hamburger menu (‚ò∞) or swipe from left edge
3. Scroll to find "Magazine" (with book icon üìñ)
4. Tap to enter Magazine

## Using Magazine

### Browsing Issues

#### Magazine Shelf
When you first open Magazine, you'll see:
- **Current Issue** (large featured card at top)
- **Past Issues** (scrollable list below)
- **Submit Ad** button (orange FAB in bottom-right)
- **Info button** (‚ìò in top-right for feature explanation)

#### Opening an Issue
- Tap any issue card to view its contents
- Each issue contains 10-30 curated ads
- No infinite scroll - finite, editorial experience

### Interacting with Ads

Each ad card supports three gestures:

#### 1. Double-Tap ‚Üí Circle
- **How**: Quickly tap twice anywhere on the card
- **What it does**: Marks the ad as interesting
- **Visual feedback**: Orange circle animation, "Circled" badge appears
- **Use case**: Save ads you want to remember

#### 2. Swipe Left or Right ‚Üí Crumple
- **How**: Swipe the card horizontally (either direction)
- **What it does**: Dismisses the ad
- **Visual feedback**: Card crumples and fades away
- **Use case**: Remove ads you're not interested in
- **Note**: Card is removed from view permanently (for this session)

#### 3. Swipe Down ‚Üí Tear Off
- **How**: Swipe the card downward
- **What it does**: Opens full ad details
- **Visual feedback**: Card tears away downward
- **Use case**: Learn more about the advertiser

### Viewing Ad Details

After "tearing off" an ad:
- See full headline and description
- View sponsor information
- Browse image gallery (if available)
- Tap "Learn More" / CTA button to visit advertiser's website
- Tap back arrow to return to issue

**Safety Note**: External links open only when you explicitly tap the CTA button.

### Submitting Your Own Ad

#### Accessing the Form
1. From Magazine Shelf, tap the "Submit Ad" button (bottom-right)
2. Or find it in the Magazine menu

#### Filling Out the Form
Required fields:
- **Brand/Creator Name**: Your business or project name
- **Headline**: Catchy title (max 60 characters)
- **Description**: Tell your story (max 500 characters)
- **Category**: Choose from dropdown (App, Art, Local, Event, etc.)
- **Image**: Upload a photo (recommended 1200x800px)
- **Website**: Your destination link
- **Contact Email**: For editorial team communication

#### Submitting
1. Fill all required fields
2. "Submit for Review" button becomes enabled
3. Tap to submit
4. See confirmation dialog
5. Editorial team reviews within 5-7 business days

**Note**: Not all submissions are guaranteed publication.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I scroll forever?**  
A: Magazine is intentionally finite. Each issue contains a curated selection, like a real magazine. This prevents ad fatigue and maintains quality.

**Q: Will ads appear in my timeline?**  
A: No. Never. Magazine is completely separate. Ads only appear when you visit Magazine.

**Q: How do I find my circled ads?**  
A: Currently, circled ads show a badge on the card. A dedicated collection view is planned for future updates.

**Q: Can I undo a crumple?**  
A: Not in the current session. The ad disappears from view. It will reappear if you close and reopen the issue.

**Q: How long until my submission is reviewed?**  
A: Editorial team reviews within 5-7 business days. You'll receive an email update.

**Q: Are submissions free?**  
A: Submission is free. Standard rates apply for approved placements.

**Q: Can I edit a submission after sending?**  
A: Not currently. Contact the editorial team via your submission email if you need changes.

**Q: Why are there no videos?**  
A: Magazine focuses on static, editorial content to avoid autoplay and data usage concerns.
